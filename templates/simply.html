FEATURES OF SplitNSave

SplitNSave simplifies the process with three powerful features:

Bill Splitter: No more awkward calculations or confusion when splitting bills! This feature allows you to effortlessly divide expenses between friends, ensuring everyone pays their fair share. You can add expenses, adjust amounts, and even calculate tips to make settling up quick and easy.

Trip Expense Calculator: Planning a getaway? SplitNSave helps you budget and manage your trip expenses. From transportation to meals, this feature lets you track costs and splits, so you can easily see how much each person owes for the entire trip, keeping everyone on the same page.

Bucket List Savings: Dreaming of a future goal? Whether it's a vacation, a new gadget, or a special event, the Bucket List Savings tool helps you set savings goals, track progress, and stay motivated. SplitNSave lets you allocate funds to different goals, making it easier to plan and save for your dreams.

Travel Planner:This feature offers a free, interactive travel planner that generates a day-long itinerary based on your entered destination and budget.Distributes your budget across activities like breakfast, sightseeing, lunch, movies, snacks, and transport using predefined weights.
